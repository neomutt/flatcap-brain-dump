DYK 035 — Attach keyword examples

Common attach_keyword examples to help detect mentions of attachments in multiple languages:
set attach_keyword = "\\<(attach|attached|attachments?)\\>"
# or add translations: "\\<(bijvoegen|bijgevoegd|bijlage|anhängen|angehängt)\\>"
Combine with abort_noattach = yes to warn when you mention attachments but attach nothing.
