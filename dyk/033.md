Use folder-hooks and display_filter to trim repetitive or irrelevant sections from automated emails (like JIRA). Example folder-hook with sed to remove long boilerplate:
folder-hook JIRA 'set display_filter = "sed -n \/^SUMMARY\/,\/^ENDSUMMARY\/-p"'
Tune the sed/grep expression to match your email source.
