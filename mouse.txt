	Mouse commands requiring the CTRL modifier can be simulated by typing
	the "g" key before using the mouse:
	    "g<LeftMouse>"  is "<C-LeftMouse>	(jump to tag under mouse click)
	    "g<RightMouse>" is "<C-RightMouse>	("CTRL-T")
